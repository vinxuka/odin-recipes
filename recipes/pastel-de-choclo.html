Pastel de Choclo

You may wonder "What is this?". Well "Pastel de Choclo is a gift from the Culinary Gods. It's a recipe made with smashed grains of corn and spices on top. On the bottom of this, there's an awesome mix of meat, onion, boiled eggs and more spices!.

Pastel de Choclo it's a fresh recipe that you can eat with Tomato Salad.

Ingredients

  • 10 Cups of frozen grain of corn
  • 2 spoons of butter
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • Some basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • 1 Spoon of cornstarch deluded in 1/4 of water
  • 2 Spoons of vegetal oil
  • 2 pounds of chopped up beef meat
  • 1 Cup of water
  • 1/2 spoon of ground cumin
  • 2 spoons of multipurpose flour
  • 3 medium chopped up Onions
  • 8 olives
  • 2 boiled eggs
  • 1 spoon of sugar

How to Pastel de Choclo

  1. For the fill, put some oil in a cooking pot, turn the fire on and put the chopped up meat on it. Put some salt and pepper on it, put some paprika and the cumin for 6 minutes. Now put the Onion, and let the meat cook for 30 minutes. Consider stir the mix. Then cook for another 30 minutes. Now put some flour.
  2. Now for the upper part, put some butter in a pot, turn the fire on and put the corn, along the Salt, pepper, milk, paprika, basil and cook for 10 minutes. Then put all in a food processor till it becomes some kind of paste.
  3. Damn this is getting long right?, don't worry we are almost done. Put some chopped up boiled eggs into the meat mix, now add the olive.
  4. Cover the meat mix with our corn paste in some recipient.
  5. Put some sugar on top, and put it on the oven with 190Â°C for 30 minutes
