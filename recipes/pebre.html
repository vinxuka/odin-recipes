Pebre

What is Pebre?

Pebre is some kind of sauce that's very popular in Chile. You can try it with rice, meat, and even with bread. It's very easy to do!.

Ingredients

  • 1 Fresh big tomatoes chopped up on small cubes
  • 2 Medium Yellow onion chopped up on small cubes
  • 2 Garlic cloves chopped up
  • 3 Spoons of chopped up parsley leaves
  • 3 Spoons of chopped up coriander leaves
  • 1 Green chili chopped up
  • 1 spoon of red chili sauce
  • 2 spoons of vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper as your wish!

How to pebre

  1. Just mix all the ingredients!
  2. Let the pebre rest for 30 minutes, add salt or pepper if necessary
  3. Serve with rice, bread or whatever you want to try

That's it!, the best things are often that simple, right?.
