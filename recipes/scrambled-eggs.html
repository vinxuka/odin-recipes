Scrambled Eggs

Delicious scrambled eggs for lazy ppl like you and me

Do you wonder what's the most efficient way to start the morning?, well, eggs are the answer my friend . This are not only the best breakfast but the most simple and easy to do.

Awesome to start the day, and the best way to eat bread!.

Ingredients!

  • Eggs (3 recommended)
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Optional: Chess, bacon.

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat a skillet in medium heat
  2. Put some butter to it
  3. Put the heat on low
  4. Put the eggs and start to stir
  5. Add Salt
  6. You can put some Cheese and Bacon before the eggs if you want to
  7. Continue to stir and don't let the eggs become to dry

That's it. You can enjoy the eggs with some nice bread and coffee/tea
